## Welcome!

Are you interested in studying a semester abroad at Code & Context as an exchange student?  
Code & Context (CoCo) is a interdisciplinary field of study that combines coding, design and entrepreneurship.

It is a study at TH Köln (Cologne University of Applied Sciences) located in Cologne Mülheim, on Schanzenstraße [22](https://maps.app.goo.gl/rC2F8SXVdq2U4SXQA) and [28](https://maps.app.goo.gl/PDM3jmiTxSuaiDiB9).

This website is for international exchange students planning on studying abroad in Germany and want to come to Code & Context. So here you will find the details on what the study Code & Context is like.  
For more general information take a look at the [Exchange Students page of the TH Köln](https://www.th-koeln.de/en/international_office/exchange-students_21380.php), there you will find more broader information for any study at TH Köln and information regarding Visa and similar.
