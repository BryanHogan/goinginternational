## Welcome!

Are you interested in studying a semester abroad at Code & Context?  
Code & Context (CoCo) is a interdisciplinary field of study that combines coding, design and entrepreneurship.

It is a study at TH Köln (Cologne University of Applied Sciences) located in Cologne Mülheim, on Schanzenstraße 22 and 28.

This website is for international student planning on studying abroad in Germany and want to come to Code & Context. So here you will find the details on what the study Code & Context is like.  
For more general information take a look at the Exchange Students page of the TH Köln, there you will find more broader information for any study at TH Köln and information regarding Visa and similar.
