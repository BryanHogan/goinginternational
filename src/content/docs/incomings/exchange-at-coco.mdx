Code & Context (CoCo) is one of many studies available at TH Köln (Cologne University of Applied Sciences).

CoCo has its own location and is a bit different from other studies at TH Köln.

If you want more general information on any study at TH Köln go to their [official page for incoming exchange students](https://www.th-koeln.de/en/international_office/exchange-students_21380.php).
